Sformato

Description

It is a typical winter dish and can be made with many different ingredients

Ingredients

  • Potatoes
  • Cheese
  • Ham

Steps

  1. Peel the potatoes. Dice and cook in salted water until they can be easily pierced with a fork but are not falling apart.
  2. Combine potatoes with eggs, Parmigiano and milk. Mix well. Add salt and pepper to taste.
  3. Press about 60% of the potato mixture onto the bottom and up the sides of the pan.
  4. Layer the prosciutto and scamorza inside, making two alternating layers of each and cover with more mixture.
  5. Bake at 350Â°F for approximately one hour, until golden, adding the rosemary sprig to the top of the sformato after about 20 minutes of baking.
